How to Take Screen Shot on your MacBook

Basic Screenshot:

  1. Press these three keys simultaneasouly: SHIFT + COMMAND + 3

  2. A thumbnail will pop up in the lower right corner, click on it to edit or it will save to your desktop after a couple of seconds.

Cropped Screenshot:

  1. Press these three keys simultaneasouly: SHIFT + COMMAND + 4

  2. Your mouse will turn into a crosshair. Now you will select the area you want to screenshot. To cancel this press 'ESC'

  3. To take the picture you release the mouse or trackpad.

  4. A thumbnail will pop up in the lower right corner, click on it to edit or it will save to your desktop after a couple of seconds.

Screen Recording:

  1. Press these three keys simultaneasouly: SHIFT + COMMAND + 5

  2. This will pop up the control bar which will give you the options to screen shot, cropped screenshot, record your screen, or record a portion of your screen.

  3. Click the option you want and your recording will begin.

  4. To stop click the stop button or press 'Command + Control + ESC'

  5. Use the tools in the thumbnail to edit how you see fit
